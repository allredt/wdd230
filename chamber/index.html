Current Events

Please join us for a Ribbon Cutting and Business After Hours to celebrate the Grand Opening of the Westerra Credit Union branch located at 4231 B South Buckley Road, Aurora, CO 80013. Westerra Credit Union holds approximately 2 billion in assets and serves 123,000 members throughout Denver metro area. Founded in 1934 by eight schoolteachers, today they carry that purpose forward to help individuals, families, and businesses. In the past decade, Westerra has donated more than $500,000 annually to the community through sponsorships, donations, and grants. Enjoy wine, beer, food, music, cornhole, a drawing, and giveaways!
